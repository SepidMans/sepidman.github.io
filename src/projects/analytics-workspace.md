## The challenge

Analytics products often ask people to interpret dense information while navigating too many competing panels, filters, and metrics at once. This concept explores how a workspace can feel more supportive by reducing noise and giving each section a clearer role.

## The direction

I focused on a modular dashboard structure with a visible reading order, stronger spacing, and calmer grouping between navigation, summary metrics, and detailed views. The goal was not to remove complexity entirely, but to present it in a way that feels manageable.

## What I would refine next

The next iteration would include stronger task-based views, a more deliberate filter experience, and usability testing around how quickly users can identify primary actions.
