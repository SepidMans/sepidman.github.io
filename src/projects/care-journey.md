## The challenge

Care-related services often combine urgency, uncertainty, and emotionally sensitive decisions. When the interface feels abrupt or overly administrative, people can lose confidence quickly.

## The direction

This concept explores a calmer service journey using step-by-step guidance, softer content tone, and clearer feedback at every stage. The design intent is to make each choice feel understandable without making the experience feel cold.

## What I would refine next

I would test the pacing of the flow, the clarity of support messages, and the balance between empathy and efficiency in the interface language.
